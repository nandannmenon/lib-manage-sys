Our Vision & Mission

SNU Library serves as an excellent, modern knowledge resource centre, disseminating the latest information for teaching, learning, and research. We maintain effective national and international resource-sharing networks while implementing best management practices and encouraging lifelong learning.

Library Services

Core Services

  • Printing, scanning, and photocopying services
  • Online database access through LAN
  • Network resource sharing through DELNET
  • Interlibrary loan services

Collections

  • Reference Collection
  • Textbooks Collection
  • Light Reading Collection
  • 14 Newspapers (English & Hindi)

Library Rules & Privileges

Loan Privileges

  • Faculty: 10 Books (6 Months)
  • Post Graduates: 10 Books (4 Weeks)
  • Under Graduates: 5 Books (2 Weeks)
  • Staff: 5 Books (6 Months)

Important Rules

  • Maintain silence in library premises
  • Submit bags at baggage counter
  • Do not reshelf books - leave on reading tables
  • Report damaged books before borrowing
